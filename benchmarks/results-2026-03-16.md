Timestamps are near-identical between engines (max delta ~0.02s on word 11/12).

## Conclusion

mlx-whisper is **4.2x faster** than faster-whisper on Apple Silicon with cached models. Output quality (segments, words, timestamps) is identical. **Phase 2 implementation is justified.**
